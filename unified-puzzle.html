    How to Play Uploadle

    Welcome to Uploadle! Test your YouTube and pop culture knowledge by guessing when popular videos were uploaded.

    Your Goal: Guess the correct month and year when each video was uploaded.

    How it works:

    • You'll see a video thumbnail, title, view count, and channel info
    • Select the month and year you think the video was uploaded
    • You have up to 4 guesses per video
    • Your guesses will be color-coded to help you:
      • Green: Exact match!
      • Yellow: Close (within 2 months or within 1 year)
      • Gray: Not close

    Daily Challenge: There are 5 videos to guess each day. Try to get them all right!
